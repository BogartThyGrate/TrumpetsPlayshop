About Us

Trumpets Playshop

Playshop is Trumpets’ workshop and training program that sets the stage for emerging and world class talent in the performing arts. Since its humble beginnings in 1993, Playshop has proven to be a home where aspiring performers of all ages discover and explore their talents.

Trumpets Playshop is an innovator in developing workshops and pioneering courses such as Street Dancing, Musical Theatre, Public Speaking, Modeling, and Hosting. It also offers advanced classes under Trumpets Playhouse and the Artist Development Program. Each class will be led by industry professionals who lend their knowhow, passion, and valuable theater experiences to develop the student’s skills in the performing arts. All this is done in the spirit of God, and good wholesome fun! The year-round workshops culminate in a much anticipated showcase festival prepared by the students and teachers.

Trumpets Inc

Established in 1991, Trumpets is Asia’s premier Christian theater company. Trumpets has become known for critically and popularly acclaimed original productions: JOSEPH THE DREAMER, FABLES AND PARABLES, SO DAVID, N.O.A.H (NO ORDINARY AQUATIC HABITAT), the Filipino musical PAMILYA MALETA, and a hit concert entitled THE SOUND OF TRUMPETS.

Trumpets is also known for its outstanding adaptations of classic tales such as: C.S. Lewis’ THE LION, THE WITCH, AND THE WARDROBE and THE HORSE AND HIS BOY; Hans Christian Andersen’s THE LITTLE MERMAID; Oscar Wilde’s THE YOUNG KING, and Maurice Maeterlinck’s THE BLUEBIRD OF HAPPINESS. Trumpets’ mission is to not only develop world-class productions, but to teach, inspire, entertain, and to change lives through theater.
