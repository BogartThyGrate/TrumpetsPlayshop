Toddler's Hip Hop

3-6 years old
Toddler's Hip-Hop is the most exciting and energetic class for your little ones. Our experienced instructors will teach your child the basic moves of hip-hop through fun games and activities. Your little one will have a blast dancing to upbeat music and choreography while learning rhythm and coordination.

Dance Class for Kids

7-12 years old
Trumpets Playshop’s Dance Class for Kids tackles different dance forms such as street dancing and hip-hop. Dance/Choreography may vary by the day and/or based on the student’s needs.

Dance Class for Teens

13 - 17 years old
Our Dance Class for Teens provides students with a space to express themselves through movement, build a community, and become the dancers they want to be. Our teachers encourage students to explore their dancing ability and artistry no matter what their skill level is.
