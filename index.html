About Us

Trumpets Playshop offers workshops and training programs that allow aspiring performers to explore and enhance their skills -- singing, dancing, acting, and more. As an innovator in developing workshop programs, Playshop has pioneered courses such as Street Dancing, Musical Theater, Public Speaking, Modeling, and Hosting. Playshop also offers advanced classes under Trumpets Playhouse and the Artist Development Program for students who wish to launch a career in Theater.
